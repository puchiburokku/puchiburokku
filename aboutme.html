About Me

Hi there! I’m Niki, a passionate Petite Block builder and all-around enthusiast for creative, hands-on hobbies. If you’ve ever found joy in assembling tiny pieces into something amazing, you’re in the right place.

This blog is my little corner of the internet where I share my adventures in building Daiso’s Petite Blocks. From adorable animals and quirky sea creatures to playful dinosaurs, I dive into every kit with curiosity and creativity. I try to write detailed reviews to help us in the petite blocks community to decide which sets to try, sprinkle in tips here and there to spark imaginations, and even share step-by-step guides to make the process easier for everyone.

What I love most about Petite Blocks is the freedom to make each build your own. I believe in thinking outside the box—sometimes literally! Whether it’s customizing a Velociraptor’s pose, turning a Tyrannosaurus Baby into a surprise Easter egg, or creating your own tiny diorama, there’s endless potential in these tiny blocks.

When I’m not building mini projects, you can find me translating Japanese videos related to my favourite artists here at my livejournal. Come and visit if you're a fan of KinKi Kids! A lot of english fansubbed content awaits!

If you have any petite blocks that you want me to review or even just to share your excitement about petite blocks, then head on up to my X account and DM me! Here! @nikilikespuchiburokku
